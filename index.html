Best Practices Team

Our purpose ("Why?")

We help TYPO3 developers build high-quality extensions and sites.

Best Practices Team

Our mission ("What?")

  • We keep evaluating and adding new helpful tools and examples for best practices to the Tea example extension.
  • Also, we keep current with new versions of PHP, TYPO3, Symfony etc.
  • We also build and maintain a project builder template for TYPO3 extensions.

Best Practices Team

What's in it for you?

  • You will learn how to ensure high quality for extensions
  • Help others to build high quality extensions
  • Support docs.typo3.org with tested code examples

Best Practices Team

Requirements for joining the team

  • You have reviewed 2 PRs by the current team members.
  • You have got 2 of your non-trivial PRs in our projects merged.
  • You have been present in 3 consecutive team calls.
  • You express your wish to join our team, to further out purpose/vision/mission, accept the team rules, and are willing to invest some hours per month for this team.

Documentation Team

Our purpose ("Why?")

Up-to-date documentation is important for a product like TYPO3.

Documentation Team

Our mission ("What?")

  • Comprehensive, relevant, up-to-date and easy to find documentation about TYPO3 products for all users.
  • Provide the TYPO3 community with the tools to improve documentation.
  • Provide information about how to write documentation.
  • Provide infrastructure and support to help the TYPO3 community write great documentation.

Documentation Team

What's in it for you?

  • Writing documentation and reviewing pull requests is a way to learn more about the different parts of TYPO3.
  • Improve your English, if you are not a native speaker.

Documentation Team

Requirements for joining the team

  • You should be able to dedicate time to working on the docs regularly and take responsibility for providing effective documentation for the community.
  • Optional: You are interested in the tools and want to improve them with PHP or frontend/JavaScript development.

Motivation Research Team

Oliver Klee, Sabine Schmidt

Motivation Research Team

Our purpose ("Why?")

We want to make work in volunteer communities (like the TYPO3 community) more engaging, motivating and fun. This helps those communities grow and thrive.

It also contributes to building trust and a feeling of connection between the members.

Motivation Research Team

Our mission ("What?")

  • becoming experts on motivation psychology
    and gamification
  • providing advice to other teams (if requested)
  • building ready-to-use tools to enhance motivation

Motivation Research Team

What's in it for you?

  • You learn a lot about motivation psychology and gamification, which you can use in your job.
  • You get to nerd out on game design and psychology together with like-minded people.
  • You will have a good time in a very friendly,
    high-performance team with a high degree of psychological safety.

Motivation Research Team

Requirements for joining the team

  • You want to become an expert on motivation psychology and gamification.
  • You like playing computer games or parlor games.
  • You are willing to invest at least 4 hours per month into the team.
  • You are fine with our team culture and team rules.
